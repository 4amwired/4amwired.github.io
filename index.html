replace colors with #000000 (for black) with the hexadecimal system. Each of the 6 hexadecimal digits represents two colors (2 each for R,G,B. 0 is the lowest and represents an absence of color. F is the highest and represents maximum brightness. You can also shorten hex code (eg: #FF0000 = #F00).
